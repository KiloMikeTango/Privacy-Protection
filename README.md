# Shield

Protect your privacy by covering chosen apps with a simple lock screen.

## What It Does
- Puts a clean white screen over selected apps until you unlock
- Lets you hide the app icon for extra privacy

## Features
- Choose which apps to protect
- Unlock with a tap pattern (your secret sequence)
- Hide or show the Shield app icon
- Quiet and lightweight

## How To Use
1. Open Shield
2. Set your secret tap pattern
3. Allow the prompts so Shield can work
4. Pick the apps you want to protect
5. Press the shield button to start
6. When a protected app opens, tap your pattern on the screen to unlock

## If You Hide The Icon
- Open Shield by typing `shield://open` in your browser’s address bar

## Tips
- You can add or remove protected apps anytime
- If the cover doesn’t appear, reopen Shield and check the prompts

## Privacy
- Your pattern and app choices stay on your device
